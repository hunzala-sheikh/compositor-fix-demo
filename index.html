What Was Fixed

Root cause: The ornamental golden ring is a thick decorative band (~r=850 to ~r=960 in Im0 space) with crown/floral motifs at the cardinal points. The original compositor used a circle at r=945, which extended deep into this ring band, overwriting the inner decorations with AI art.

The fix: Reduce the art circle radius so it stays entirely inside the ring:

  • Geometric circle tightened from r=945 to r=830–850 (inside the innermost ring decoration)
  • Hybrid mask min(circle, smask) still handles outer scrollwork
  • All ring ornaments (beads, crowns, floral motifs) preserved pixel-perfect
